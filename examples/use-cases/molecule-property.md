# Molecule Property Prediction

Graph neural networks for molecular property prediction (e.g., drug activity, toxicity) are powerful tools in modern drug discovery. Sharing these models across organizations and research collaborations is increasingly valuable — but requires confidence that the underlying compound data stays protected. LeakPro helps teams proactively assess privacy exposure, supporting safe model sharing and enabling compliance documentation for IP-sensitive research partnerships.
